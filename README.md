## Project Overview
This project is an interactive **HR Analytics Dashboard** built using **Power BI** to analyze employee attrition and workforce trends.  
It helps HR teams understand **why employees leave**, identify **high-risk groups**, and take **data-driven decisions**.

## Key Metrics
- Total Employees: 1,470  
- Attrition Count: 237  
- Attrition Rate: 16.1%  
- Average Salary: 6.5K  
- Average Age: 36.9 Years  
- Average Experience: 7 Years  

## Dashboard Analysis
- Attrition by Age Group  
- Attrition by Salary Slab  
- Attrition by Education  
- Attrition by Gender  
- Attrition by Job Role  
- Attrition by Department  
- Attrition Trend by Years at Company
